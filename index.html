Listing Description

Apartment in the exclusive Aquamarina residential complex a few meters from the sea in Manilva. It has 3 bedrooms, a delightful living room, a full equipped kitchen with a separated cleaning room and a gorgeous terrace with pleasing views. On top of that, the apartment is fitted with big garden areas that contain a paddle tennis court, a swimming pool, a fantastic playground all assisted with the help of a concierge all year around.
